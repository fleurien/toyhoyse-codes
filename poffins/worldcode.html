An invasive species known through the galaxy for their skills in mimicry and imitation, Poffins are a small kemonomimi race distinguished by their butterfly like ears and false eyes. While in the modern day these function mostly as decoration, in the past (and in dangerous situations) these false eyes served to distract from the Poffin and protect them from predators, much like eyespots in butterflies and moths.

Poffins have a wide variety of differences within their traits, allowing them to evolve to better match their planet/s of choice. Evolution typically happens quickly and within a few generations; this being one of the notable reasons that their species has spread so successfully.

Rules
  1. No hate speech or discrimination will be tolerated. Treat all users with respect.
  2. Poffins may not be used to promote or appear in art that is racist, anti-LGBTQA+, anti-semitic, bigoted, or otherwise intentionally hateful/harmful content.
  3. Rule 2 does not apply to things like drug use, dark themes, and otherwise mature/NSFW content (18+ users/characters only).
  4. Poffins may not be heavily based off of existing copyrighted characters. Light inspiration is fine.
  5. Do not copy existing Poffins or characters for your design.
  6. Scamming of any kind is a bannable offense.
  7. Poffins may not be sold, traded, gifted, or otherwise transferred to blacklisted users.
